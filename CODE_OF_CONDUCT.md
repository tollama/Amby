# Code Of Conduct

Amby follows a simple contributor standard: be respectful, specific, and focused on the work.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Be direct without being dismissive.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experience levels.
- Keep criticism technical, specific, and actionable.
- Avoid sharing private data, secrets, raw prompts, model responses, or security exploit details in public channels.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Disruptive conduct that prevents productive project work.
- Public disclosure of vulnerabilities before maintainers have a reasonable chance to triage.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or limit participation when behavior conflicts with this code. Security reports should follow `SECURITY.md`.
